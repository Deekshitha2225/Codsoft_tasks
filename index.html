About Organ Donation

Organ donation is a noble act of saving lives. By donating your organs, you can give a second chance at life to those in need.

Benefits of Organ Donation

Save Lives

One donor can save up to eight lives.

Improve Quality of Life

Organ donation can improve the quality of life for recipients.

Be a Hero

Organ donors are considered heroes by those they help.

How to Become an Organ Donor

Becoming an organ donor is easy. Follow these steps to register:

  • Register online at the national organ donor registry.
  • Inform your family about your decision.
  • Carry an organ donor card.
